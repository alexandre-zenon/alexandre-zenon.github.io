GRADIT

Group Resilience and Adaptation to Diverse Individual Traits

Objectives: To explore how the psychological traits of individual members influence their wellbeing in collective life, and the dynamics of intentional communities.

  • Which traits favor good integration within community life?
  • Which traits make adaptation difficult?
  • Which traits support cooperation, equity, and long-term community stability?
  • Which traits may lead to tension, power struggles, or community breakdown?
  • How can communities recognize and respond to these patterns to maintain power balance, trust, and inclusion?
  • How do different communities in various cultural contexts adapt to individual differences?
